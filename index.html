Lili Zhao

My research develops tools for analyzing medical image data, aiming to provide a better understanding of artificial intelligence image representation and understanding design of digital expert systems. My research often falls at the intersections of Machine Learning and Patter Recognition, Medical Image Processing and Analysis.

I'm joining the School of Information Science and Technology in Nantong University, starting September 2017. I am currently an academic visitor at University of Leicester, mentored by Huiyu Zhou.

Prospective Students

I'm recruiting M.S. students to start Fall 2023 at Nantong University! I'm especially looking for students with a background and/or interest in artificial intelligence, patter recognition, machine learning, image segmentation, or image classification, and are passionate about using computational methods to study how online public medical images can be resegmented or classification with limited labeled data. If you are interested, please apply to the School of Information Science and Technology M.S. program and mention me in your application. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out by email (ylzh@ntu.edu.cn)!
